Creator. Connector. Thinker. Dreamer.

Hi! My name is Kelly Huang and I am a 2nd-generation Taiwanese-American born & raised in the amazing city of Austin, Texas! Welcome to my website where I share bits & pieces of my personal & professional work, experiences, and issues that I care about. Some of my interests include, but are not limited to: health & fitness, anything creative (art, music, dance, design), cooking & baking, intersectionality, mental health awareness, and Asian-American identity topics.

I believe that we are all perpetual students in this amazing journey called life, and that every human should have the equal opportunities to education, peace, happiness, and love. I hope that my content encourages, comforts, empowers, and inspires you to make your life the way you dream it to be.
